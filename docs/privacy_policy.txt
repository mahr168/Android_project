PRIVACY POLICY
Diabetes Risk Analyzer
Version 1.0
Effective Date: 2025

================================================================
1. INTRODUCTION
================================================================

This Privacy Policy describes how the Diabetes Risk Analyzer
application ("the App") handles information provided by users.

By using this application, you agree to the terms described in
this Privacy Policy.

================================================================
2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
================================================================

The Diabetes Risk Analyzer app may collect the following
information that you voluntarily provide:

- Full Name (optional)
- Age
- Body Mass Index (BMI)
- Fasting Glucose Level
- Symptom responses (water intake, urination frequency,
  weight loss, wound healing time, hunger level)

================================================================
3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
================================================================

All information entered into the app is used ONLY for:

- Calculating your diabetes risk level
- Storing your result history locally on your device
- Displaying past results in the History screen

Your data is NEVER used for:
- Advertising or marketing purposes
- Selling to third parties
- Analytics or tracking
- Any commercial purpose

================================================================
4. DATA STORAGE
================================================================

All data entered in this application is stored LOCALLY on your
device only using Android Room Database (SQLite).

- No data is transmitted to any external server
- No data is uploaded to the internet
- No cloud storage is used
- No Firebase or remote database is connected

Your data remains entirely on your device and under your control.

================================================================
5. DATA SHARING
================================================================

We do NOT share your personal information with:

- Third-party companies
- Advertisers
- Government agencies
- Any other individuals or organizations

The only exception is if you voluntarily use the "Share Result"
feature, which allows you to share your result text via apps
of your own choice (WhatsApp, Email, etc.).

================================================================
6. DATA SECURITY
================================================================

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security
of your data depends on your device security settings.

We recommend:
- Using a screen lock on your device
- Keeping your device software updated
- Not sharing your device with untrusted individuals

================================================================
7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
================================================================

This app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children under
the age of 13 without parental consent.

================================================================
8. MEDICAL DISCLAIMER
================================================================

IMPORTANT: This application is developed for EDUCATIONAL and
AWARENESS purposes only.

The risk assessment provided by this app:
- Is based on a simplified algorithm
- Is NOT a medical diagnosis
- Should NOT replace professional medical advice
- Should NOT be used for medical treatment decisions

Always consult a qualified and licensed healthcare professional
for proper medical evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
