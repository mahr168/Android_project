1. INTRODUCTION
================================================================

Diabetes Risk Analyzer is an Android application designed to
estimate your diabetes risk level based on clinical health
data and common diabetes symptoms. The app uses a Fuzzy Logic
Inspired Algorithm to provide an instant risk assessment.

The app analyzes the following inputs:
- Patient name (optional)
- Age
- Body Mass Index (BMI)
- Fasting Glucose Level (mg/dL)
- 5 symptom-based questions

================================================================

3. INSTALLATION GUIDE
================================================================

METHOD A — Install from APK file:

Step 1: Download the APK file from the GitHub release page
Step 2: Open your device File Manager
Step 3: Navigate to the Downloads folder
Step 4: Tap on DiabetesRiskAnalyzer.apk
Step 5: If prompted, tap "Settings" and enable
        "Install from Unknown Sources"
Step 6: Tap "Install"
Step 7: Tap "Open" when installation completes

METHOD B — Run from Android Studio:

Step 1: Clone the project from GitHub
Step 2: Open Android Studio
Step 3: Open the project folder
Step 4: Wait for Gradle sync to complete
Step 5: Connect your Android device or start an emulator
Step 6: Click the Run button (green triangle)

5. STEP-BY-STEP USAGE GUIDE
================================================================

STEP 1 — OPEN THE APP
   Launch Diabetes Risk Analyzer from your app drawer
   The splash screen appears for 2-3 seconds
   The main input screen opens automatically

STEP 2 — ENTER PATIENT INFORMATION
   Full Name: Type your name (this field is optional)
   Age: Enter your age in years (e.g. 35)
   BMI: Enter your Body Mass Index (e.g. 24.5)
        (BMI = weight in kg / height in meters squared)
   Glucose: Enter your fasting blood glucose level in mg/dL
        Reference values shown below the field:
        Normal: less than 100 mg/dL
        Pre-diabetic: 100 to 125 mg/dL
        Diabetic: 126 mg/dL and above

STEP 3 — ANSWER 5 SYMPTOM QUESTIONS
   For each question, select ONE option that best
   describes your current condition:

   Q1 - Daily Water Intake:
        Select how much water you drink per day

   Q2 - Urination Frequency:
        Select how often you urinate per day

   Q3 - Weight Loss (Last 6 Months):
        Select how much weight you have lost recently

   Q4 - Wound Healing Time:
        Select how long your wounds typically take to heal

   Q5 - Hunger Level:
        Select how you would describe your hunger level

   NOTE: The progress bar at the top shows how many
   questions you have answered (0/5 to 5/5)

STEP 4 — TAP ANALYZE
   Once all fields are filled and all 5 questions answered,
   tap the "ANALYZE MY RISK" button at the bottom
   
   If any field is empty, an error message will appear
   pointing to the specific missing field

STEP 5 — VIEW YOUR RESULTS
   The Result screen opens automatically
   
   SUMMARY TAB shows:
   - Risk level (LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH) with color
   - Total score out of 26
   - Risk meter bar
   - Symptom score and clinical bonus breakdown
   - Patient information summary
   - Personalized recommendation message
   - Notification message
   - Per-question score breakdown with bars

   CHART TAB shows:
   - Bar chart comparing scores for all 5 symptoms
   - Radar chart showing your overall risk profile

STEP 6 — SHARE YOUR RESULT (Optional)
   Tap the Share icon in the top right corner
   Choose any app (WhatsApp, Email, etc.) to share

STEP 7 — VIEW HISTORY
   Tap the History icon (clock) in the top menu
   All previous results are listed with date and time
   Tap "Clear All" from menu to delete all history

6. UNDERSTANDING YOUR RESULTS
================================================================

RISK LEVELS EXPLAINED:

GREEN — LOW RISK (Score 7 or below)
   Your responses indicate normal health indicators
   Recommendation: Maintain your healthy lifestyle
   Continue regular exercise and balanced diet
   Schedule routine annual health check-ups

YELLOW — MEDIUM RISK (Score 8 to 14)
   Some warning signs have been detected
   Recommendation: Take action now
   Reduce sugar and processed food intake
   Increase physical activity (30 min/day)
   Monitor your blood sugar regularly
   Consider consulting a healthcare professional

RED — HIGH RISK (Score 15 or above)
   Multiple significant risk factors detected
   Recommendation: See a doctor IMMEDIATELY
   Do not delay professional medical evaluation
   Get a proper fasting blood glucose test done
   Follow doctor's advice strictly

SCORE BREAKDOWN:
   Base Score: Sum of your 5 symptom answers (max 20)
   Clinical Bonus: Additional points for age, glucose, BMI
   Total Score: Base + Bonus (max 26)

7. HISTORY FEATURE
================================================================

The app automatically saves every analysis you complete.

To VIEW history:
   Tap the History icon in the top action bar

Each history record shows:
   - Patient name
   - Date and time of analysis
   - Risk level (color coded)
   - Total score
   - Glucose level, BMI and Age

To DELETE history:
   Open History screen
   Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
   Tap "Clear All"
   Confirm deletion

NOTE: History is stored locally on your device.
Uninstalling the app will delete all history.

================================================================
8. NOTIFICATIONS
================================================================

After every analysis, the app sends a push notification
to your device status bar.

Notification types:
   LOW RISK:    Normal notification with green checkmark
   MEDIUM RISK: Warning notification with yellow alert
   HIGH RISK:   Urgent alert with red emergency symbol

To enable notifications (Android 13+):
   When prompted, tap "Allow" for notification permission
   Or go to Phone Settings > Apps > Diabetes Risk Analyzer
   > Notifications > Enable All

9. TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================

PROBLEM: App crashes on opening
SOLUTION: Restart your device and try again
          Make sure you have Android 7.0 or higher

PROBLEM: Analyze button not working
SOLUTION: Make sure ALL 5 symptom questions are answered
          Make sure age, glucose, and BMI fields are filled
          Check for red error messages under each field

PROBLEM: No notification appearing
SOLUTION: Check notification permission in phone settings
          Go to Settings > Apps > Diabetes Risk Analyzer
          Enable Notifications

PROBLEM: History screen is empty
SOLUTION: You need to complete at least one analysis first
          History saves automatically after each analysis

PROBLEM: Charts not showing
SOLUTION: Swipe to the Chart tab on the Result screen
          Charts animate automatically after 1 second

PROBLEM: BMI field — what value to enter
SOLUTION: Calculate BMI = weight(kg) / height(m) squared
          Example: 70 kg, 1.75 m tall
          BMI = 70 / (1.75 x 1.75) = 22.9
